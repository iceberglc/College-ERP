## Full Description (4000 chars max)
```
ICEBERG LC is the official student management app for Iceberg Language Center.

Stay on top of your studies with everything in one place:

📚 DAILY VOCABULARY
• Study today's word list assigned by your teacher
• Flashcard mode — tap to reveal the translation
• Built-in quiz — test yourself and track your score
• See your learning streak and progress over time

📅 ATTENDANCE
• View your attendance record for every class
• Check present, late, and absent sessions at a glance
• Weekly trend chart so you always know where you stand
• Interactive monthly calendar view

📝 ASSIGNMENTS
• See all homework assigned by your teacher
• Submit your work directly from the app
• Track pending and completed tasks

📊 RESULTS & PROGRESS
• View your test and exam scores by group
• Score history chart to track improvement
• Performance trend across attendance, homework, and quizzes

🔔 NOTIFICATIONS
• Instant alerts when teachers post new vocabulary
• Assignment reminders and updates
• All notifications in one inbox

👤 YOUR PROFILE
• Manage your account and settings
• Light and dark mode support

ICEBERG LC is designed to make studying more organized, more motivating, and more effective — right from your phone.

Download now and take control of your learning journey.
```
